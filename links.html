  • Useful course aids
    Wolfram Alpha
    WolframAlpha knows everything. Absolute monster in solving equations, determining properties of elements, mechanical data of alloys, stock exchange lines, and providing pikachu graphs. Other websites on this list only provides a neater interface for displaying information that is present in Wolfram's extensive database.
    WebElements
    Advanced periodic table that contains information like crystal structures, thermodynamics, atomic sizes, and other properties important in your courses.
    Quick Math
    Plot, solve, factor, integrate, differentiate, and work with matrices online.
    MIT Materials Science and Engineering Open CourseWare
    Notes and references for materials engineering courses
    SmallPDF
    A quick, neat little website that manages pdf files really well. Merge and split any number of pdf files, and compress them to save space.
    Griddy
    Figure out your timetable and your professors before you sign up for the courses. Hit the program button on the top right to quickly get you started.
    ECF remote access
    Access the ECF windows computers outside of campus. Very useful when you need to work with your files, or enter access restricted resources.
    MSE101 - Scott Ramsay
    This site is intended to serve as a learning resource to students of materials science, and contains various example problems and descriptions of important topics in materials science.
